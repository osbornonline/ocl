About Us

A New Contractor, Built on Proven Foundations

Osborn Contractors was established by the team behind Osborn Associates, a multidisciplinary engineering consultancy with over 32 years of delivering complex projects across the UK and internationally.

That depth of experience in project management, building services and construction oversight now underpins everything we do as contractors. We understand how projects are delivered from every angle - ensuring better coordination, tighter control and consistently high standards on site.
